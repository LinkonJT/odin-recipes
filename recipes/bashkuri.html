Bashkuri Cooking Recipe

Description

Bashkuri is traditional tripura indegenous food. It can be cooked in so many different ways with different flavours. It could be cooked with Dal, Traditional Dry fish pest, and also with other traditional food items. Most of the time Bashkuri is cooked without oil and only using boiled water. Thus, it is also a healthy food item to have on your daily diet list.

Ingrediants

  • Bashkuri
  • Chilli
  • Oil 1ts, Salt 1ts
  • water, Merma, etc

Steps

  1. first boil the Bashkuri in water for 5 min
  2. Add napi 1Table spoon and boil for 4 min
  3. add chilli, ginger, salt
  4. add perseley and cover the top for 2min
  5. Enjoy your Delicious Bashkuri meal
